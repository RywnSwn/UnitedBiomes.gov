About the United Biomes of the Overworld

Our nation, our mission, and our future.

🏛

Our Mission

The United Biomes of the Overworld is a sovereign nation established to unite all peoples of the Overworld under a single, organized government. We believe in structured civilization, fair governance, and the right of every citizen to build, trade, and thrive without fear of chaos or outside aggression.

As a young and growing nation, the United Biomes is committed to expanding its territory, infrastructure, and institutions to serve its citizens for generations to come.

🌎

Territory

The United Biomes of the Overworld currently governs a sovereign landmass in the Overworld. Exact territorial boundaries are subject to expansion as the nation grows and new lands are claimed and settled under U.B. jurisdiction.

All land within U.B. borders is protected under national law. Unauthorized entry, trespassing, or hostile action against U.B. territory is considered an act of aggression against the state.

📌
Border Infrastructure

A national border wall and gate system is currently a planned infrastructure project to formally define and secure U.B. territory. Updates will be posted as construction begins.

Planned National Projects

The United Biomes has a number of major national infrastructure and institutional projects currently in planning. These projects represent the long term vision of the U.B. government.

Planned
U.B. Federal Court

The national judicial institution of the United Biomes. Responsible for interpreting U.B. law and resolving disputes between citizens and the state.

Planned
United Biomes Reserve (UBR)

The central bank of the United Biomes of the Overworld. The UBR will manage the national currency, oversee financial stability, and support economic growth across U.B. territory.

Active
Nether Embassy & Highway

The U.B. Nether Embassy serves as the nation's diplomatic and transit presence in the Nether dimension, anchored by direct access to the Nether highway network.

Planned
Minecraft Transit Authority (MTA)

A U.B. governed national rail network connecting all major cities across the Overworld. One of the most ambitious infrastructure projects in U.B. history, the MTA is a long term commitment to connecting every corner of the nation.

Planned
National Border Wall & Gate

A fortified border wall encircling U.B. territory with official entry and exit points. Designed to formally define national borders and regulate who enters the United Biomes.

Planned
U.B. National Insurance Program

Details to be determined. A government program to provide citizens with coverage and support. More information coming soon.
