About the United Biomes

The United Biomes of the Overworld is a sovereign Minecraft nation committed to the peaceful unification of all biomes under a single democratic government. Founded on the principles of freedom, fair resource distribution, and mutual defense against hostile mobs, the UBO continues to grow in both territory and population.
