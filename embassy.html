About the Embassy

The U.B. Nether Embassy serves as the official presence of the United Biomes of the Overworld in the Nether dimension. Built from materials imported from the Overworld, the embassy stands as a symbol of U.B. sovereignty and civilization in one of the most hostile environments known to Minecraft.

The embassy serves as the primary hub for highway access, citizenship registration, and visa processing for travelers and residents operating in or near U.B. territory.

Highway

Nether Highway Access

The U.B. Nether Embassy is directly connected to the Nether highway network, providing fast overworld travel for citizens and authorized travelers. The highway is maintained and governed by the United Biomes of the Overworld.

📌
Embassy Entrance

The highway entrance is located at the embassy. Coordinates will be published upon the server's founding.

🛡
Authorized Access Only

Use of the U.B. Nether highway is restricted to U.B. citizens and holders of a valid travel visa.

⚠
Travel Advisory

The Nether is an extremely dangerous environment. The U.B. government is not responsible for deaths occurring outside embassy grounds.

Visas

Visa Program

The United Biomes of the Overworld operates a visa program for non-citizens who wish to travel through, reside in, or conduct business within U.B. territory. All visa applications are processed at the Nether Embassy.

Type A Travel Visa

For non-citizens who wish to pass through U.B. territory or access the Nether highway network.

  • Valid reason for travel
  • Embassy personnel approval
  • Compliance with U.B. law during transit
Type B Residency Visa

For non-citizens who wish to build, live, or operate a business within U.B. borders for an approved period of time.

  • No explosive devices of any kind
  • No illegal potions or potion drugs
  • Full compliance with U.B. law
  • Subject to periodic embassy review
  • Approval may be revoked at any time
Note: Visa holders who fail to meet requirements will be given notice to vacate U.B. territory. The U.B. government reserves the right to deny or revoke any visa without appeal.
Citizenship

Citizenship Registration

The Nether Embassy serves as the official processing center for U.B. citizenship applications. All approved citizens are recorded in the official U.B. Citizenship Registry, a Book and Quill maintained at the embassy, and displayed on the Embassy Registry Board.
