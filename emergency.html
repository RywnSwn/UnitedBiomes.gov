🚨 Citizen Emergency Help

Official emergency guidance issued by the U.B. Department of Defense.

⚠ IMPORTANT This page provides official U.B. government guidance only. The government cannot intervene in real time. Citizens are advised to act quickly and use their best judgment in all emergency situations.
⚔️

Hostile Player / PVP Threat

High Threat

If you are being pursued or attacked by a hostile player, your primary objective is to disengage and return to the safety of U.B. borders immediately. The United Biomes of the Overworld will not tolerate unprovoked aggression against its citizens.

Recommended Protocol

1
Disengage Immediately

Do not engage the hostile player unless absolutely necessary. Turn and flee toward U.B. territory.

2
Return to U.B. Borders

Make your way back inside U.B. borders as quickly as possible. You are under the protection of U.B. law within national territory.

3
Report the Incident

Once safe, report the incident to embassy personnel. Include the hostile player's name and the circumstances of the attack.

📌
Note

Unprovoked attacks on U.B. citizens may be treated as an act of aggression against the state and handled accordingly by the U.B. government.

💥

Griefing / Act of Terrorism

Critical Threat

Griefing of U.B. territory, citizen property, or national infrastructure is classified as a terrorist act against the United Biomes of the Overworld and will be treated with the full severity of U.B. law. Any deliberate destruction of U.B. property is an attack on the state.

Recommended Protocol

1
Ensure Your Safety First

If the griefer is still present and poses a physical threat, disengage and follow hostile player protocols above.

2
Document the Damage

Take screenshots of all damage caused. This serves as official evidence for the U.B. government's response.

3
Report to Embassy Personnel

Report the act of terrorism to embassy personnel immediately. Provide all evidence including screenshots and the perpetrator's name if known.

4
Do Not Retaliate Unilaterally

Allow the U.B. government to handle the situation. Unauthorized retaliation may complicate the government's response.

🌎

Lost / No Coordinates

Medium Threat

Getting lost outside U.B. borders is a serious situation, particularly at night or in hostile biomes. Follow these steps to find your way back safely.

Recommended Protocol

1
Check Your Coordinates

Press F3 to open the debug screen. Your exact coordinates are displayed on the left side of the screen. Note them down.

2
Find the Nether Highway

If you have access to a Nether portal, enter the Nether and locate the highway network. The U.B. Nether Embassy is accessible via the highway.

3
Seek Shelter Immediately

If night is approaching and you cannot find your way back, build or find shelter. Do not travel in the open at night.

4
Contact Embassy Personnel

If you are unable to find your way back, contact embassy personnel with your F3 coordinates for assistance.

📚

Lost Items / Death

Low Threat

The United Biomes of the Overworld acknowledges the unfortunate reality of item loss due to death, lava, or other circumstances. The government offers the following official guidance.

⚠ Official Government Notice

The United Biomes of the Overworld and its affiliated institutions are not liable for any items lost as a result of death, environmental hazards, hostile players, or any other in-game event. All items are the sole responsibility of the citizen who owns them.

Recommended Protocol

1
Return to Death Location Quickly

Items despawn after 5 minutes. Return to where you died as fast as possible.

2
Note Your Death Coordinates

Your death coordinates are displayed on the death screen. Write them down before respawning.

3
Accept Your Losses

If items are gone, they are gone. The U.B. government expresses its condolences. Please gear up and carry on.
