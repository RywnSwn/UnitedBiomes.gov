Become a Citizen

Citizenship in the United Biomes of the Overworld is open to all players who meet the requirements and receive presidential approval. Citizens are entitled to the full protections and rights of U.B. law, access to national infrastructure, and the right to reside within U.B. borders.

Requirements

  • ✅
    Agree to U.B. Law

    All citizens must agree to abide by the laws and regulations of the United Biomes of the Overworld.

  • ✅
    Presidential Approval

    Citizenship must be approved by the President of the United Biomes. The President reserves the right to deny any application.

  • ✅
    Present Yourself at the Nether Embassy

    Applicants must appear in person at the U.B. Nether Embassy to complete the citizenship process.

The Process

1
Travel to the Nether Embassy

Make your way to the U.B. Nether Embassy via the Nether highway. Embassy coordinates are listed below.

2
Present Yourself to the President

Speak with the President of the United Biomes and express your intent to become a citizen.

3
Agree to U.B. Law

Formally agree to abide by all laws and regulations of the United Biomes of the Overworld.

4
Registry Enrollment

Upon approval, your name will be recorded in the official U.B. Citizenship Registry, a Book and Quill maintained at the Nether Embassy, and added to the Embassy Registry Board.

U.B. Citizenship Registry

The United Biomes of the Overworld maintains an official Citizenship Registry, a Book and Quill record kept at the Nether Embassy. All approved citizens are recorded in this registry and displayed on the Embassy Registry Board.

The registry is maintained and updated by the Office of the President. It serves as the official record of all recognized citizens of the United Biomes.
